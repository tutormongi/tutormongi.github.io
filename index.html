About Me

I'm Mongi, a passionate Qur’an, Arabic, and Islamic studies teacher with over 3 years of experience. I have worked with various academies like Alimlive, Quran Arabi, and Dar-Almuqamah, and I hold multiple diplomas from esteemed institutions such as Muslimeto.

Courses Offered

  • Qur’an Learning

    I offer Qur'an recitation (Tilawah) with proper pronunciation and rhythm, Tajweed to refine articulation and beautify recitation, and Memorization (Hifz) to help students commit the Qur'an to heart with accuracy.

  • Qur’anic Arabic

    I help students recognize and pronounce letters correctly, develop reading fluency, and understand key words in the Qur’an.

  • Islamic Studies

    I teach Basic Aqeedah and Fiqh to build a strong foundation in faith, Stories of the Prophets for valuable lessons, and Daily Islamic Practices to apply Islam in everyday life.
